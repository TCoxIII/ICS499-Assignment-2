## Executive Summary

This project explores Customer Relationship Management systems using generative AI as a research assistant. The assignment includes CRM research, product comparisons, open-source CRM exploration, and a proposed CRM architecture.

## AI Tools Used

The primary AI tool used was ChatGPT. ChatGPT was used to generate research prompts, organize CRM concepts, compare CRM products, and help draft architecture sections.

## CRM Research Findings

CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management. CRM systems help organizations manage customer information, track communication, organize leads, support customer service, and generate reports. CRM can be viewed as both a business strategy and a software system.

## CRM Product Comparisons

Commercial CRM systems compared include Salesforce, HubSpot CRM, Zoho CRM, Microsoft Dynamics 365, and Oracle CRM. Open-source CRM systems compared include SuiteCRM, EspoCRM, Odoo CRM, and Vtiger CRM.

## Open Source CRM Evaluation

The open-source CRM selected for exploration was EspoCRM. It was explored using an online demo environment. Screenshots were captured for the login page, dashboard, contacts, leads, and reports pages.

## CRM Architecture Proposal

The proposed CRM architecture uses HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, PHP, and MySQL. The system includes modules for authentication, contacts, accounts, leads, opportunities, tasks, reports, customer support, user administration, and audit logging.

## Prompt Engineering Examples

Prompt examples and AI responses are documented in `prompts_used.md`. Prompts were used for CRM definitions, product comparisons, architecture planning, database design, security considerations, and reflection.

## Lessons Learned

This assignment showed that CRM systems combine business processes with software features such as databases, dashboards, user roles, reporting, and workflow automation. Generative AI was helpful for organizing the research, but the information still needed to be reviewed and verified.
